        🎒 KLAUS'S PRE-SCHOOL RATIONS
        Hidden Veggie Monster Muffins: Blend 2 cups fresh spinach, 1/3 cup honey, 1 banana, and 1/3 cup melted coconut oil. Fold into 2 cups whole wheat flour and 1 tsp baking soda. Bake at 375°F for 15 mins. (Freezes perfectly for lunchboxes).
        Turkey Pinwheel Boxes: Take 4 large flour tortillas. Spread heavily with cream cheese. Layer exact strips of deli turkey and thinly shredded carrots. Roll extremely tight, slice into 1-inch pinwheels. Store in bento boxes.
        ⚡️ HIGH-ENERGY SNACKS
        Oat & Hemp Power Bites: Mix 2 cups rolled oats, 1/2 cup peanut butter, 1/3 cup honey, 1 tbsp chia seeds, and 1/4 cup hemp hearts. Chill 30 mins, roll into bite-sized spheres.
        Safe Scouter Packs: Wash and slice 3 bell peppers. Halve grapes/cherry tomatoes *vertically* (crucial for the 2yo choking hazard). Store in airtight glass containers with a damp paper towel to retain crunch.
